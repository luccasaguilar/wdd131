CEC / FSW / FST

Canadian Experience Class (CEC)

For skilled workers with Canadian work experience who want to become permanent residents.

Federal Skilled Worker Program (FSW)

For skilled workers with foreign work experience who want to become permanent residents.

Federal Skilled Trades Program (FST)

For skilled workers qualified in a skilled trade who want to become permanent residents.

Sponsor your family members

Spouse, Partner or Dependent Children

Bring your spouse, partner or dependent children to Canada as permanent residents.

Adopted Children

Bring your adopted child to Canada as a permanent resident.

Other Relatives

Sponsor certain relatives to come to Canada as permanent residents.

Parents and Grandparents

Bring your parents and grandparents to Canada as permanent residents.

Provincial Nominee Program (PNP)

This program is for workers who:

  • Have the skills, education and work experience to contribute to a specific province or territory
  • Want to live in that province or territory
  • Want to become permanent residents of Canada

Each province and territory has its own “streams” (immigration programs that target certain groups) and requirements. These may include:

  • Students
  • Business people
  • Skilled workers
  • Semi-skilled workers
