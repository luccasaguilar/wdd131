Express Entry

Express Entry is a system used by the Canadian government to manage applications for permanent residence from skilled workers. It includes programs like the Federal Skilled Worker Program, Canadian Experience Class, and more.

Family Sponsorship

The Family Sponsorship Program allows Canadian citizens and permanent residents to sponsor their relatives to come to Canada as permanent residents. This includes spouses, children, parents, and grandparents.

Provincial Nominee Program (PNP)

PNPs allow provinces and territories to nominate individuals who wish to immigrate to Canada and who are interested in settling in a particular province. Each province has its own criteria and streams.
